Koh Samui

Koh Samui is a resort island in the Gulf of Thailand that offers beautiful beaches and various activities. Visitors range from budget travelers staying in beachside bungalows to wealthy holidaymakers enjoying 5-star resorts and villas.

Pattaya

The coastal city of Pattaya, Thailand has been a popular tourist destination since the 1980s. Its beaches are constantly bustling with watersports enthusiasts and sun-seekers. Pattaya is renowned for its scuba diving opportunities, with calm waters and fascinating shipwrecks to explore year-round.

Phuket

Phuket is a top beach destination with beautiful beaches, palm trees, and exciting nightlife. There are many accommodation and dining options to choose from, as well as opportunities for island-hopping and cruising around the stunning Phang Nga Bay.
