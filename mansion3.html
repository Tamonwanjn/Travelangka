Location on map

Kamala, Thailand

Villa Saan sits within the exclusive Cape Amarin estate on the ?Millionaires' Mile' headland separating Kamala and Patong beaches. With panoramic sea and sunset views, the villa overlooks a small bay, where the roofs of some of Phuket's finest resorts and villas peep through the tropical treetops that border the ocean. Just six kilometres to the north sits Kamala Beach, a pretty stretch of soft golden sand popular with families. Try surfing, body boarding, simply relaxing on the sand, or indulge in some Thai seafood (or pizza!) at one of the casual open-air restaurants lining the beach. A bit further away to the south is the hectic Patong Beach, with a good range of water sports, shopping, dining and nightlife choices.
